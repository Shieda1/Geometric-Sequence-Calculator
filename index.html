What is the geometric sequence?
The geometric sequence is a sequence of non-zero numbers where each term after the first is found by multiplying the previous one by a fixed, non-zero number called the common ratio. It is also called the geometric progression.
How to calculate geometric sequence?
To calculate geometric sequence you should use the following formula:
a + a*r + a*r^2 + a*r^3 + .......
where:
a = start term
r = common ratio
Why use geometric sequence?
Geometric sequence is important because it can be used as a model of real life situations which can find it's application in physics.
